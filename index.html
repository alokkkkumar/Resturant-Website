Desserts

Nan Khatai

Naankhatai is a popular traditional Indian shortbread cookie, cherished for its rich, buttery taste and distinctive melt-in-your-mouth, crumbly texture. These sweet, aromatic biscuits are typically made with flour, ghee (clarified butter), sugar, and often flavored with cardamom, then baked to a delicate golden perfection.

RS-100

Ice Cream Kulfi

Kulfi is a traditional Indian frozen dessert, distinct from conventional ice cream due to its preparation method. Unlike churned ice cream, kulfi is made by slowly simmering milk until it thickens and reduces, resulting in a much denser, creamier, and icicle-free texture. It's typically frozen in molds and comes in rich, intense flavors like pistachio, mango, cardamom, saffron, and malai.

RS-80
